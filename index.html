Our Purpose

To create a safe physical and online environment for everyone.

The purpose of making a real-time face detection system is to detect and identify human faces in real-time video or image streams

Real-time face detection can be used to enhance security and surveillance systems, such as monitoring for intruders or identifying potential threats in public places.

Real-time face detection can enable more natural and intuitive human-computer interaction. For example, it can be used to recognize facial expressions and gestures, or to personalize user experiences based on a person's identity.

Real-time face detection can be used in retail settings to analyze customer behavior and optimize store layouts. For example, it can be used to track customer movements and determine which products are attracting the most attention.
Safety of people is very important. This is our goal and purpose.
